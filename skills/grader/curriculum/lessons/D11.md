# Hallucination / Safety Guards

High-stakes output needs explicit permission to say "I do not know." Without it, models often prefer a plausible guess over an honest gap.

Add one guard sentence and, when the stakes are high, a verification step. A self-check against the source material is often enough to catch fabrications before you act on them.

- Try this: Add "If you are unsure or the data is insufficient, say so rather than guessing. Then list which claims you can verify from the source."
