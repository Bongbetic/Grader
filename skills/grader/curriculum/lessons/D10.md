# Voice: Persona / Style / Tone / Audience

Voice matters when the output is read by a human. Style is what the text looks like, tone is how it feels, and audience is who receives it. Treat them separately.

Avoid caricature personas such as "world-renowned expert who never makes mistakes." They add fluff and set impossible expectations. Name the perspective you actually want.
