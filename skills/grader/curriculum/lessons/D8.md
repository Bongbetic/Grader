# Grounding / Reference Material

When factual accuracy matters, never rely on the model's memory. Ground the answer in source material you supply or retrieve.

Dumping every related document hurts more than helping. Scope the reference to the most relevant passages, and ask the model to cite or flag uncertainty. That habit directly cuts hallucination.

- Try this: Paste the key paragraph and add: "Base your answer only on the text below. If it is not covered, say so."
